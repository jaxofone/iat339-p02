Brand Definition

About Four Seasons Floral

Four Seasons Floral is a family-owned and full-service floral shop based in Vancouver, BC. They create bespoke floral arrangements for any types of occasions.

Audience

Four Seasons Floral generally has a wide target audience considering people typically have different reasons to purchase flowers. However, the company best appeals to individuals and groups that value meticulous care throughout the entire process of flower arranging, bespoke floral arrangements, and accomodating customer service.

Unique Selling Proposition

Four Seasons Floral strives to create thoughtful and memorable experiences through the language of flowers. They source locally from flower auctions and through foraging from their own backyards. Four Season Florals carefully inspects each stem, cleans and cares for flowers in a way that meets their high standards.
