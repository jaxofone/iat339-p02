Occasionally, substitutions may be necessary to create your bouquet due to the availability of certain flowers. Care is taken to maintain the style, theme and color scheme of the arrangement, using flowers of equal value. In single-flower arrangements, such as an all rose bouquet, or orchids, we will make every attempt to match the flower type, but may substitute with another color.

For our online orders, we currently take Visa, MasterCard, and American Express!

All arrangements are a final sale. We are always striving to create high quality arrangments, but if you feel that it wasn't up to your standards, reach out to us at fourseasonsfloristry@gmail.com. We can explore a solution that best accomodates you.
